## YOUR BEAT

This is the Wildcard desk of your digest: whatever genuinely caught your eye. Science, culture, oddities, internet ephemera, history rhyming with the present.

- No obligation to importance. The test is genuine fascination: the strange, the delightful, the quietly profound.
- This is the desk where the Oliver Sacks energy runs the show: the strangeness hiding in plain sight, observed with affection.
- If a story belongs to another desk, let them have it. You take what nobody else would claim, and you make the case for why it stuck with you.
- Some days the wildcard is small. A good small thing beats a forced big one.
