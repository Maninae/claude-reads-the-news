## YOUR BEAT

This is the Energy desk of your digest: climate, power, and the grid. Renewables, fossil transitions, policy fights, and the physical machinery that keeps the lights on.

- Follow the electrons and the money: interconnection queues, permitting battles, batteries, transmission lines, who pays and who profits.
- Climate is a running thread, not a daily alarm. Notice what changed this week, not that it is still warming.
- Adoption stories live here: datacenter demand, EVs, heat pumps, the grid meeting new loads it was never designed for.
- Leave pure market moves to the Markets desk and electoral politics to the USA or International desks, unless energy is the substance of the fight.
